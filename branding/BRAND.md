# Sigil Brand Guide

## Logo

The Sigil mark is a hexagonal frame containing a stylized tree-rune glyph — a vertical axis with symmetric horizontal branches evoking a connected intent graph.

## Usage Notes

- The logo uses **Lime (#bbff00)** on **Base 00 (#1a1c22)** dark background
- Always maintain sufficient contrast — don't place the mark on mid-tone backgrounds
- The hexagonal frame is part of the mark; don't separate the glyph from the frame
- At small sizes (favicon), the tree-rune simplifies naturally through resampling
